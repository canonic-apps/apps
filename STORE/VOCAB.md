## Duality Terms

**WRITING**: Production governance. Creates artifacts.

**READING**: Consumption governance. Distributes artifacts.

**PRODUCER**: Creates governed artifacts via WRITING.

**CONSUMER**: Accesses governed artifacts via READING (STORE).

---

## Store Terms

**STORE**: The certified app distribution registry. Primary READING interface.

**CERTIFICATION**: Attestation that an app passed domain validation.

**CERTIFICATION HASH**: Cryptographic proof of certification at a version.

**REVOCATION**: Withdrawal of certification, propagates to all consumers.

---

## App Categories

**SYSTEM APP**: Operates on the CANONIC platform itself. Examples: AGENT, EVOLUTION.

**PLATFORM APP**: Integrates external platforms under governance. Examples: EMAIL, TWITTER, LINKEDIN.

**DOMAIN APP**: Certified for a specific professional domain. Examples: MAMMOCHAT (medicine), GENOMICS (genomics).

---

## Agent Terms

**AGENT**: Personal agent to act on your behalf in the canonverse.

**ORCHESTRATION**: AGENT coordinates multiple apps under unified governance.

**DELEGATION**: User grants AGENT permission to act on their behalf.
