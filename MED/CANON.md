1. MED apps MUST inherit from /CANONIC/.
2. MED apps MUST implement OPTS-EGO for provenance.
3. MED apps MUST audit to LEDGER with HIPAA compliance.
4. MED apps MUST credential operators via NPI or equivalent.
5. MED apps MUST maintain 7-year audit retention.
